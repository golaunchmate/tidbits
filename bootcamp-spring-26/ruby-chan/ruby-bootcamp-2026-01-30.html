The Interview

Transfer Student Reality

Interviewed a transfer student in her second semester at SDSU. She works with prospective students daily.

The Problem

Big clubs are easy to find. Niche programs and small communities? Nearly invisible.

"Very niche programs... not all people know of. So it's hard to find those very small communities."

She found her clubs through Admitted Student Day booths and Instagram searching — but acknowledged that's only works for the obvious ones.

2/3

Three Directions

What Emerged

  • Niche discovery: Help students find small clubs/programs that match their specific interests
  • Transfer isolation: Solve the "dropped into a huge campus" feeling without freshman orientation
  • Purposeful events: She only attends events with a point — professional, major-related, learning something

Next Step

Pick a direction and brainstorm solutions.
