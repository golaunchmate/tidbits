The Mission

The Discovery

They met Daniel

An older professional who'd been through multiple careers — producer, coffee chain owner, serial reinventor.

"I wish I knew all that when I was younger. We don't think about that, or we're not exposed to it."

Daniel shared his practice: gratitude every morning and night, always having goals, and the power of finding the right mentor.

3/5

The Problem

Young people aren't exposed to wisdom until it's too late

  • Mindset skills (gratitude, resilience) aren't taught in school
  • Mentorship is key — but young people don't know to seek it
  • Older generations have learned lessons the hard way
  • There's no bridge between the two
