The Discovery

We interviewed everyone

5 interviews. 5 completely different problem spaces:

  • Lonely journalism student wanting friends
  • Engineering students abandoned by professors
  • Nursing students who can't stand out
  • Fraternity members surviving through peer support
  • International professor paying $600/year for tax help nobody understands

We had to pick one.

2/5

The Decision

We found a real business

Amanda, an anthropology professor on an H1B visa, told us:

"Generally intelligent people with no tax comprehension" — describing her international faculty colleagues

The pain was clear:

  • ✗ Few CPAs understand H1B tax rules
  • ✗ Contradictory info online
  • ✗ Stakes are high (visa status)
  • ✓ Already paying $400-600/year

Underserved market + willingness to pay = go.

3/5

What We Built

visa-tax-help.lovable.app

A CPA matching platform for H-1B visa holders:

  • ✓ Tax savings calculator (~$8,500 potential)

  • ✓ CPA profiles with H-1B specialties
  • ✓ Trust signals and testimonials
