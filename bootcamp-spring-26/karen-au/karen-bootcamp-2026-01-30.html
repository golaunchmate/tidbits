Where I Started

Three Problems, One Decision

Went out and interviewed students. Came back with three real pain points:

  • Parking indicators — knowing where spots are available
  • Electrical outlets — finding places to charge on campus
  • Study spaces — finding the right spot to focus

All real problems. But which one could we actually solve today?

2/5

What I Discovered

The Commuter's Hidden Struggle

Talked to a commuter student in Theatre, Film & Media. What they said:

"I need corner spots with more privacy, sitting next to only one person, near a window with natural light. It's easier to focus in quieter spots."

Their current solution? Walk to the library and hope.

Most of the time they settle for a noisy, distracting environment because their ideal spots are taken.

The pain isn't studying. The pain is everything BEFORE studying.

3/5

The Real Opportunity

Three Ways to Solve It

1. Spot Scout

Real-time info on premium spots before you leave. Know your ideal spot is open.

2. Spot Match

Crowdsourced map of hidden gems tagged by preference: quiet, window, corner, private.

3. Spot Reserve

Reservation system for premium spots. Claim your corner before you arrive.
