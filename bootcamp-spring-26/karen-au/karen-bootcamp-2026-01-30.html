The Problem

Not Just Any Spot—The Right Spot

Interviewed a commuter student in Theatre, Film & Media. What they need:

"Corner spots with more privacy. Sitting next to only one person. Near a window with natural light. Quieter spots where it's easier to focus."

Their current approach? Walk to the library and hope.

Most days they settle for a noisy, distracting spot because their ideal ones are taken.

2/4

The Insight

The Pain Is Before Studying

Studying itself is fine. The friction is everything that happens before:

→ Walking to library hoping

→ Scanning for the right spot

→ Finding ideal spots taken

→ Settling for less

This is PRE-consumption friction. The opportunity is helping students know where their ideal spot is before they start searching.

3/4

Solution Approaches

Three Directions

Spot Scout

Real-time info on premium spots. Know your ideal spot is open before you leave.

Spot Match

Crowdsourced map of hidden gems tagged by preference: quiet, window, corner, private.

Spot Reserve

Reservation system for premium spots. Claim your corner before you arrive.
