The Problem We Chose

The Insight

New students and commuters miss campus events because flyers and A-frames don't reach them — and info stays siloed within interest groups.

Current solutions fail:

✗ Flyers = limited physical reach

✗ Instagram = algorithm buries posts

✗ SDSU calendar = clunky, no one checks it

✗ Word of mouth = only reaches existing networks

Who feels it most: New students + commuters

3/5

A visual map dashboard showing campus event hotspots in real-time.

No more wandering. No more FOMO. Just open the map and see where to go.
