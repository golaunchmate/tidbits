The Strategic Choice

Go Upstream

Instead of fixing math struggles in college, prevent them in middle school.

Problem Statement

"Students check out of math when it stops feeling relevant."

  • Before class: Cultural baggage, "math is hard" messaging
  • During: Abstract, no connection to real life
  • After: Bad grade = "I'm not a math person"
3/5

The Concept

Celebrity Math Videos

AI-generated videos of celebrities kids actually care about — Ariana Grande, Jake Paul — teaching math in contexts that matter to them.

  • Algebra through gaming stats
  • Fractions through music production
  • Geometry through fashion design

Target

Elementary through high school

Risks Identified

Legal (deepfake rights) • Authenticity • Educational rigor
