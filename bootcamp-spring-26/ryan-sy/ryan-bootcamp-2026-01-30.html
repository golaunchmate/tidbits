The Real Opportunity

Pre-Health Scheduling Chaos

Students on pre-health tracks without dedicated majors (pre-PA, pre-med, pre-dental) are flying blind.

The problem isn't "student life" — it's:

  • PRE: Which classes even count for my path?
  • CONSUMPTION: Racing to register before they fill
  • POST: Realizing you picked wrong, scrambling to fix

Narrower target. Clearer pain. Real urgency.

4/5

Where I'm At

Next Step

Going back to interviews to find the sharpest angle before building solutions.
