Where I Started

I interviewed a guy stuck climbing 5 floors

Nick lives on the 5th floor. Both elevators have been broken since December. Management sends AI-generated non-answers.

He tried everything — door-to-door organizing, collecting emails, even talking to a lawyer.

"They just see us as money. They don't see us as real people."

The lawyer told him there's nothing in his lease about elevator access. Dead end.

2/5

What I Discovered

The lease isn't the only angle

Nick asked the wrong question. He asked about his lease. He should have asked about ADA, building codes, and fire safety laws.

Most tenants don't know what leverage they actually have.

The Real Opportunity

TenantShield

An app that helps renters discover hidden legal leverage and access lawyers they can actually afford.

  • Find laws you didn't know applied (ADA, building codes, habitability)
  • Get a case strength assessment before wasting time
  • Match with tenant-rated lawyers (reviews BY renters, FOR renters)
  • Crowdfund legal fees with neighbors — 40 people × $50 = real lawyer

Business model: Commission on lawyer matches + small platform fee on crowdfunding
