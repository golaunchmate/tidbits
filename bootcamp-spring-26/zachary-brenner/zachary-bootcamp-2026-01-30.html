Where I Started

Looking for a real problem

Went into discovery interviews with no preconceived ideas. Found a marketing coordinator at SDSU's College of Business who runs three major conferences including the Dean's Conference for all business deans in California.

Asked her one question: "How do you stay organized?"

The Dean's Conference spans UCSD, USD, and CSU systems. Every institution uses different tools—Google Drive, Dropbox, various platforms. Files get lost. Formats break.
