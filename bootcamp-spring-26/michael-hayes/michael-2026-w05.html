The Decision

One agent per student, tagged by team

We considered shared team agents, but landed on individual agents with team tags. Each student gets their own context, their own memory, their own conversation history.

Why it matters

When a student asks for help, the agent knows their problem, their interview notes, their solution approaches. Not mixed with 4 teammates.

The tidbit skill gets embedded in each agent too—so at the end of bootcamp, every student can generate their own progress summary.

3/5

The Risks

Three systems that could buckle

Vercel — Can our serverless functions handle 80 concurrent users? Craig's funding the scaling.
Letta API — 80 agents making calls simultaneously. Messaging their team about limits.
Supabase — Database reads/writes with everyone logged in at once.

I'm point person on making sure none of these break during the event.
