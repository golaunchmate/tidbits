The 0 → 1 Insight

CPAs sell hours. We sell outcomes.

Conventional thinking: "Help CPAs find international clients."

That's 1 → n. Any marketplace could do it.

The contrarian truth:

"AI can do the tax work. CPAs just need to verify and sign. The moment you flip that, you've built something traditional firms can't copy."

Amanda doesn't want a CPA. She wants a financial translator who speaks both countries — and then the paperwork just happens.

The Model

$200 flat fee. Everyone wins.

Customer pays $200
CPA gets (to review & sign) $100

✓ Customer saves $200-400 vs current CPA
