The Origin

Started learning guitar 1.5 years ago...

  • Could play the notes, but didn't sound right
  • Every amp, pedal, guitar = different tone
  • No way to know what settings to use
  • Built solution for my own problem
Dec 8-9, 2024
First video went viral on localhost
That night
Deployed overnight, launched immediately
3 months later
64,000 users, 861 active subscribers
2/5

The Realization

"I thought people would use this every jam session."

  • Reality: They use it to learn specific songs
  • Subscribe → get their 5 tones → cancel
  • Not a retention problem, just honest usage
"It's like a reference library, not a daily tool."
3/5

The Tension

Launched Tone Database for SEO...

  • 2M views on launch content
  • 44K visitors in one week
  • But only 0.68% started trials
"Did I just give away the store for free?"
  • Researched tones = 90% accurate (free)
  • Adapted to YOUR gear = 92% accurate (paid)
  • Is 2% worth paying for?
4/5

What I'm Testing

Three paths, one decision

  • Keep database free → SEO long game, accept low conversion
  • Monetize database → protect value, double down on organic
  • Build habit → DAW integration, become session tool
"I solved my own problem. Now I'm figuring out if I solved it too well."
