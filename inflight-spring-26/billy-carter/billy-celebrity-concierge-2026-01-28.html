The Trade-Off

What scales vs. what's me

The real question isn't "how do I build a website." It's: which parts of Billy can become a system?

  • Stays founder-led: Trust, discretion, real-time judgment, relationship quality
  • Can be systematized: Booking flow, intake forms, partner deals, recurring events
Scaling doesn't mean removing myself. It means figuring out what needs to stay me versus what can be standardized or delegated.

4/5
Current Focus

Three things I'm locking in

  • Partner Subscription Model: $500/month for venues to access my client flow. 3-month contracts. I'm the distribution, they pay for access.
  • Client Data Capture: Every booking goes through an intake form. Name, email, occasion, group size. The list is my real asset.
  • Monthly Event ("The List"): Invite-only party for past clients. Keeps them engaged, creates content, shows venues what I can deliver.

Once these are in place, everything else — website, interns, pricing tiers — gets way easier to build.
