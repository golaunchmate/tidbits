The Problem

Views without visits

We hit 25K views on Instagram. But views don't pay the bills — and they don't prove anything to restaurants.

The real question:

"Can you turn views into visits?"

That's what we needed to crack. Not just attention — action.

2/5

The Engine

Six steps. Repeat forever.

  • 01Close a restaurant deal
    Pitch local spots, secure student discounts
  • 02Create content
    Film at the restaurant, post 2-3 videos
  • 03Activate clubs
    Send flyers + content to student orgs, run collabs
  • 04Students take action
    They see it, use the code, visit the restaurant
  • 05Track the proof
    Code redemptions, DMs, restaurant feedback
  • 06Use proof to close more deals
    "We drove X students to Cluck n Sip. Want in?"
3/5

The Flywheel

Each win makes the next one easier

More Deals → More Content → More Club Collabs → More Students → More Proof → Even More Deals

The cheat code:

SDSU Foodies as a recurring partner. Every time we close a deal, we plug it into the Foodies collab → instant traffic.

No rebuilding distribution from scratch. Just repeat.
