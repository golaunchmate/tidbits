Where I Started

Shelter Island Reality Check

Went to talk to repair professionals at the marina. Thought they'd see the value.

They liked the concept. But they wouldn't buy.

"This is more expensive for us in the long run. We already have proper methods."

But then they said something that clarified everything:

"For a boater? This would be priceless if it gets them out of a pinch."

2/5

What I Discovered

The Pitch Locked In

Shelter Island confirmed what Craig said Feb 20. The customer is the person stranded offshore, not the guy in the shop.

PROBLEM

We help boat owners get home when something breaks offshore.

SOLUTION

A carbon-fiber repair kit that bonds to metal in 15 minutes — no welding, no power, no skills required.

Two sentences. Done. Now I can say it in any conversation.
