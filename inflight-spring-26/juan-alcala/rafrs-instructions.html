Quick Repair Steps
1
Clean & Prep Surface
Remove loose debris. Sand the repair area until shiny. Wipe clean with dry cloth.
2
Mix Resin
Combine Part A and Part B in equal amounts. Stir thoroughly for 30 seconds.
3
Apply Resin to Surface
Brush a thin layer onto the repair area. Work it into any cracks or gaps.
4
Wrap with Carbon Fiber
Apply carbon fiber cloth over wet resin. Overlap edges by 1 inch. Saturate with more resin.
5
Wrap with Shrink Tape
Spiral wrap tightly with shrink tape. This compresses the repair and removes air bubbles.
6
Let Cure
Leave undisturbed for 8 hours. Remove tape. Sand smooth if needed. You're back in action.
⚠️ PRO TIPS
  • Surface prep is everything — the shinier, the better
  • Work fast once resin is mixed (you have ~15 min working time)
  • Wrap tighter than you think — compression = strength
  • If it's cold, cure time will be longer
