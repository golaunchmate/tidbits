What I Discovered

Different Muscles Needed

Three months into Cursor as Founding GTM, I'm building the foundation that doesn't exist yet.

The shift:

  • From following the playbook → figuring out what the playbook should be
  • From one-off deals → building repeatable systems
  • From executing → influencing product based on market feedback

This requires a different kind of customer immersion - not just selling to engineers, but understanding their workflows deeply enough to know what GTM motions will actually scale.
